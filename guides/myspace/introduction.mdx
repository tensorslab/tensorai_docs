## Overview

My Space is your personal gallery for managing all your AI creations and favorite templates.

## My Creations

### Features
- **View history**: See all your generated content
- **Download**: Save images and videos to your device
- **Regenerate**: Create new versions with the same prompt
- **Delete**: Remove unwanted creations
- **Share**: Generate shareable links

### Organization
- Filter by type: Images, Videos
- Sort by: Newest, Oldest
- View total creation count and statistics

## My Favorites

Save templates that inspire you for quick access later.

### How to Favorite
1. Browse templates on the homepage
2. Click the heart icon on any template
3. Access favorites from My Space

### Using Favorites
- Click "Try It" to generate similar content
- Use as inspiration for your own prompts

<Tip>
Favorite templates you like to build a personal inspiration library!
</Tip>

## Stats Overview

My Space displays your:
- Total creations count
- Image and video breakdown
- Total views and favorites received
